About Me

Not an actual SpaceX astronaut.

Hi there! I’m a Full-Stack Designer and Creative Leader who has spent the last 13 years honing my craft. I love solving design problems through user-centered design and powerful visual storytelling. No matter the size of a project or the resources allocated, I thrive in building design teams and systems capable of delivering results year after year.

In addition to design, I'm also a lifelong gamer and musician. I dabble in screenwriting and I love all things Space (see photo). Having multiple creative outlets to explore helps keep me inspired and hungry to learn more!

I would love to hear from you! If you're interested in learning more, please contact me or download my résumé.
