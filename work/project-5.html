Kitbash

Branding / Web Design / Development

The Problem

Kitbash is a small shop that produces big work. We needed a brand and website that would help us compete with the heavyweights in the industry. The word "kitbash" means to take pieces and parts from several different models and combine them to create something custom and unique. We wanted that uniqueness to resonate throughout the brand.

The Solution

We chose a logo that is made up of simple geometric shapes. This continues the theme of assembling something from several smaller parts. The color blue, images of factories and tools, and some of the copywriting are also used to convey a hard-working / blue-collar theme.
