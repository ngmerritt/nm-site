New JiJi Mascot Design

The 1st generation of JiJi mascots were an outdated design, expensive to ship to events and schools, and made it difficult for a performer to see and move. Our goal was to create a new design that matched our new and improved JiJi brand while also addressing these pain points.

Our team worked very closely with our vendor to solve these problems. It took a lot of planning and iterations to get the character dimensions right. We chose materials that were lightweight, breathable, and more durable. We chose to add a fan ventilation system into the mascot, hidden at the crown of the head to be invisible to children. And lastly we chose a structure that condenses down to half the size of previous mascots for shipping, saving the company a lot of money when sending the mascots across the country to events and schools.
