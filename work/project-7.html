Triumph Bank

Branding / Web Design

The Problem

Triumph was looking to update their brand to reflect their focus on growth and community. They also needed an improved user experience and a consistent look and feel for their banking and mortgage sites. (2016)

The Solution

Our team provided brand and identity packages, website redesign, and copywriting. I led our UX initiatives as we reworked the information architecture to allow for the mortgage and banking sites to be merged. We focused on removing redundancies, addressing pain points, and streamlining the overall user experience.

New identity package.

Interior design elements to reinfornce new branding inside Triumph locations.

Our team also provided ongoing advertising design for print, digital, out-of-home, and events.

In addition to the primary website, I led the design and development for the My Triumph microsite. My Triumph was an ad campaign that we created to share real stories from Triumph customers. The microsite acted as a showcase for both the written version of customer stories, and the videos shot and directed by our team. The My Triumph site would also help us measure the impact of the campaign due to its unique URL.

Triumph Bank was acquired by Simmons Bank and so our work on the website is no longer live.
