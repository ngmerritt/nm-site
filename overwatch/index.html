“What's for lunch?”

Nick Merritt (call sign: N/A) has spent the last decade honing his skills as a Designer. He wears many hats, although that’s partly because his hair is thinning…so BACK OFF!

Curiosity may have killed the cat, but it has only made Nick a more well-rounded artist. Over the years, Nick has acquired many new skills using his patented "Super Absorption™" technique. These skills include: Visual Design, UX Design, Front-end Development, Branding, Motion Graphics, and Digital Painting to name a few.

But Nick’s greatest strength may be the fact that he’s still hungry for more knowledge. He is at his best when in an environment that encourages creativity and experimentation with new ideas and design skills. I’m sure, with access to the right resources, there is no limit to what Nick can achieve!

Did I mention that Nick likes games? Nick didn’t just grow up with video games, he was shaped by them throughout his childhood and well into his adulthood. They offered artistic inspiration, an escape into new worlds, and a way to connect with friends across the continent.

Nick would be a perfect addition to any team. He’s experienced, eager to learn, and passionate about gaming and design.
